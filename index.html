Works
As a team, we've worked on various technologies and languages like Ember Js, Java, HTML, CSS, NodeJS, Python, Apache Spark, TensorFlow. We've compiled our works in a video below for easy assessment and understanding. Further down, you could see a timeline view of the features we've worked on.
  • Slack Integration
    The Zoho Subscriptions - Slack integration allows you to easily monitor your customers’ subscription payment activities. The notifications will be triggered based on payment actions and will be pushed into your Slack channels instantly.
  • MailChimp Integration
    MailChimp integration helps you to associate a MailChimp mailing list with a payment page inside Zoho Checkout.
  • Cliq Integration
    Zoho Cliq helps you stay connected and collaborate with teams at your workplace. With Cliq and Subscriptions integrated, you will receive notifications related to subscriptions and payments. Notifications will be pushed to the cliq channel mentioned while setting up the integration.
  • Square Integration
    The Square integration in our products help our customers to collect their clients’ payments via credit cards.
  • Apple pay Integration
    The Apple Pay integration in our products help our customers make checkout flows faster and more secure by using TouchID and Apple Wallet. The integration is done via Stripe Connect and the payments are routed through the stripe merchant account.
  • Import and Export
    Import and Export features allows customers to import and export their data in various formats. This also helps customers switch over to Zoho with ease.
  • User and roles
    User and roles feature allows our customers to bring in their colleagues within their organization as Users and provide them with a role based access. For example, you could invite your salesperson to the organization and give access to specific modules like customers or invoices.
  • Policy Optimization
    Policy optimization helps in finding anomalies in firewall rulesets. It also gives suggestions to mitigate the issues in firewall and boost up performance. Suggestion are based on rule reordering, rule deleting and so on.
  • Sentiment Analysis
    Sentiment analysis is a classical classification problem to find the sentiment for the given content. We can find the sentiment score and to the extend we can add further levels to sentiment range.
  • Keyword Extraction
    Tired of reading the whole content all the time? Keyword extraction is tasked with the automatic identification of terms that best describe the subject of a document.
